PA3 Part 1: Mixture-of-Experts (MoE)

Implementing Distributed MoE with Tensor and Expert Parallelism

1. Tensor Parallel (TP) - Starting Point

In TP, every rank holds a vertical shard (slice) of every expert's weights. The computation is split across ranks, and results are combined using collectives.

Your First Tasks

1

ShardedLinear Forward Pass

Implement the Allreduce or Allgather logic to reassemble the full output from column shards.

2

MoE_TP Forward Pass

Orchestrate the routed tokens through the sharded experts and combine gated outputs.

3

Expert Parallel (EP)

Implement Alltoall to route tokens to the ranks owning the full experts.

TP Advantages

  • Simpler routing (replicated)
  • Good for large layers

TP Disadvantages

  • High communication volume for collectives
